Swift Student Challenge Winner  —  Participation in WWDC23

Won the Swift Student Challenge hosted by Apple, and was invited to attend the WWDC23 conference held at Apple Park in Cupertino, where developers from all over the world gather. Participating in the conference provided opportunities to experience new things at Apple and in the United States, and to present these experiences at KWDC23.

Design and Development of Moon Phase Observation App (MellowYellows)

Designed and developed a moon observation app that visually shows the moon phases of the day using SwiftUI for the Swift Student Challenge. The app simulates the actual night sky, where stars dim as the moon approaches full moon and brighten as it becomes a new moon.

Immersive Animation Effects and Background Music

I created a more immersive environment with smooth and natural animations such as the moon rising, stars moving, and clouds gathering, simulating the actual night sky. Also, added serene and peaceful background music for a more immersive app experience.

Winning the Swift Student Challenge

Submitted MellowYellows to the Swift Student Challenge and was fortunately selected as one of the winners along with students from various countries. Additionally, I was invited to attend WWDC23 held at Apple's Cupertino headquarters in person.

Participation in WWDC23 at Apple Park

Participated in WWDC23 held at Apple Park, experiencing the announcements of new products like Vision Pro and new software like iOS, MacOS, WatchOS in person. Also, attended presentations on various development frameworks such as Swift and SwiftUI, getting to know the new features first-hand.

Conversations with Developers and Apple Teams from Around the World

At the conference, I met various developers from around the world and Apple teams, discussing newly announced products and technologies. It was an opportunity to get insights into the values and directions pursued by Apple and its fans through diverse conversations on various topics.

Presenting as a Speaker at KWDC23

After returning from the US, I participated as a speaker at KWDC23, a conference where all Apple communities in Korea gather, presenting my experiences with the Apple ecosystem and services in the US during WWDC23.

Growth from New Experiences

Everything was a fresh experience, from encountering development for the first time, developing an app I designed myself, visiting the US and Apple, talking with various stakeholders, and presenting in front of a large audience at a community event. These new experiences broadened my perspective in various aspects, not just in design and development, but also provided significant career motivation.
